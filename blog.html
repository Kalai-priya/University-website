Certifications and online programs in 2024

Certifications and online programs in 2024

1. Foundations of Artificial Intelligence - NASSCOM

The Foundations of Artificial Intelligence course offered by NASSCOM provided me with a strong conceptual understanding of AI technologies. From machine learning basics to real-world use cases, the course covered the key pillars that drive intelligent systems today. I explored AI's impact across industries and learned how data plays a crucial role in training algorithms. This certification strengthened my foundational knowledge and prepared me to delve deeper into AI-driven problem-solving.

2. Introduction to Cybersecurity - Cisco Networking Academy

Through Cisco's Introduction to Cybersecurity, I developed a solid grasp of cybersecurity principles and digital safety practices. The course emphasized how data breaches occur, common vulnerabilities, and how to build a culture of cyber awareness. I also gained exposure to tools and techniques used in defending systems from cyber threats. This certification sparked my interest in ethical hacking and reinforced the importance of digital protection in today's interconnected world.

3. Programming with Python - NPTEL

The Programming with Python course from NPTEL allowed me to strengthen my coding skills using one of the most versatile programming languages. I worked through hands-on exercises in functions, data structures, file handling, and error management. The course also included algorithmic thinking and problem-solving techniques using Python. This certification solidified my confidence in Python and improved my ability to write clean, efficient, and modular code for real-world applications.
